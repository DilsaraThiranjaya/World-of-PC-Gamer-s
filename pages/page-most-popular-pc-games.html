  1. Fortnite ( Fortnite.com )

    Fortnite is a globally popular online game developed by Epic Games, first released in 2017. It features several game modes, with the most famous being Fortnite Battle Royale, a free-to-play battle royale game where 100 players compete to be the last person or team standing. Players are dropped onto an island where they must gather resources, build structures, and engage in combat with others as the play area gradually shrinks, forcing encounters. Fortnite stands out for its unique building mechanics, which allow players to construct defensive structures on the fly, adding a strategic layer to the traditional battle royale formula.

    In addition to Battle Royale, Fortnite offers Save the World, a cooperative mode where players team up to fight off waves of enemies, and Creative Mode, where users can build their own worlds and games. The game is known for its vibrant, cartoonish art style, frequent content updates, and collaborations with popular franchises, celebrities, and events, which keep the game fresh and engaging. Fortnite has also become a cultural phenomenon, influencing not just gaming but also fashion, music, and entertainment, with millions of players and a strong presence in eSports.

    The game features different modes that cater to a wide range of playstyles. In Survival Mode, players must gather resources to build shelter, craft weapons, and survive against enemies like zombies and skeletons. Creative Mode removes these challenges, giving players unlimited resources to freely build and create without the constraints of hunger or health.
