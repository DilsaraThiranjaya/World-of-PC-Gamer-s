  • Baldur's Gate 3 ( Steam )

    Baldur's Gate 3 is an epic role-playing game developed by Larian Studios, marking the long-awaited continuation of the beloved Baldur's Gate series. Released in 2023, the game thrusts players into a dark and immersive world inspired by the Dungeons & Dragons universe. The story begins with the protagonist and several companions being infected by a mind flayer tadpole, a parasitic creature that threatens to transform them into mind flayers themselves. This dire situation sets off a grand journey to find a cure, all while uncovering a deeper and more sinister plot involving ancient powers and looming threats.

    The game features a rich, branching narrative where player choices have significant consequences, shaping the world and the fate of the characters. Players can engage in deep, turn-based combat, utilizing a wide array of abilities, spells, and tactics, which are all grounded in the Dungeons & Dragons 5th Edition ruleset. Baldur's Gate 3 also offers a robust character creation system, allowing players to customize their heroes with different races, classes, and backgrounds, enhancing the replayability of the game. Whether playing solo or in cooperative multiplayer, Baldur's Gate 3 delivers a complex, dynamic, and emotionally engaging experience that stands as a new high point for the genre.

    Release Date: August 3, 2023

    Game of the Year: 2023

    Minimum System Requirements:

    • OS: Windows 10 64-bit
    • Processor: Intel i5-4690 / AMD FX 8350
    • Memory: 8 GB RAM
    • Graphics: Nvidia GTX 970 / AMD RX 480 (4GB+ VRAM)
    • DirectX: Version 11
    • Storage: 150 GB available space

    Recommended System Requirements:

    • OS: Windows 10 64-bit
    • Processor: Intel i7-8700K / AMD Ryzen 5 3600
    • Memory: 16 GB RAM
    • Graphics: Nvidia GeForce RTX 2060 Super / AMD RX 5700 XT (8GB+ VRAM)
    • DirectX: Version 11
    • Storage: 150 GB available space

  • Elden Ring ( Steam )

    Elden Ring is an action role-playing game developed by FromSoftware and published by Bandai Namco Entertainment. Released in February 2022, the game quickly gained widespread acclaim for its rich lore, challenging gameplay, and expansive open-world design. Directed by Hidetaka Miyazaki, the creator of the Dark Souls series, and with world-building contributions from famed author George R.R. Martin, Elden Ring presents a dark, immersive fantasy world known as the Lands Between. Players take on the role of a Tarnished, an exile seeking to repair the shattered Elden Ring and become the Elden Lord, restoring order to the world.

    Elden Ring builds on the challenging combat and intricate world design that FromSoftware is known for while introducing a more open-ended structure. The game allows players to explore vast, interconnected landscapes filled with dangerous foes, hidden secrets, and powerful bosses. The freedom to approach challenges in multiple ways, whether through direct combat, stealth, or summoning spirits, adds a layer of depth and replayability. The narrative is delivered in a subtle, fragmented manner, encouraging players to piece together the story through exploration, item descriptions, and NPC interactions.

    The game has been praised for its atmospheric world, engaging combat, and the sense of discovery it fosters. Elden Ring has managed to appeal to both hardcore fans of FromSoftware's previous titles and new players alike, cementing its place as one of the standout games of its generation.

    Release Date: February 25, 2022

    Game of the Year: 2022

    Minimum System Requirements:

    • OS: Windows 10 64-bit
    • Processor: Intel Core i5-8400 or AMD Ryzen 3 3300X
    • Memory: 12 GB RAM
    • Graphics: NVIDIA GeForce GTX 1060, 3 GB or AMD Radeon RX 580, 4 GB
    • DirectX: Version 12
    • Storage: 60 GB available space

    Recommended System Requirements:

    • OS: Windows 10/11 (64-bit)
    • Processor: Intel Core i7-8700K or AMD Ryzen 5 3600X
    • Memory: 16 GB RAM
    • Graphics: NVIDIA GeForce GTX 1070, 8 GB or AMD Radeon RX Vega 56, 8 GB
    • DirectX: Version 12
    • Storage: 60 GB available space

  • It Takes Two ( Steam )

    It Takes Two is a co-op action-adventure platformer developed by Hazelight Studios and published by Electronic Arts. Released in March 2021, the game has been praised for its innovative gameplay mechanics and emotional storytelling. Designed specifically for two players, It Takes Two follows the story of Cody and May, a married couple on the brink of divorce who are magically transformed into dolls by their daughter’s tears. Trapped in a fantastical world, they must work together to overcome various challenges, rediscover their love for each other, and ultimately return to their human forms.

    The game is known for its seamless blend of different gameplay genres, offering everything from platforming and puzzles to racing and shooting elements. Each level introduces new mechanics that require both players to collaborate closely, with each character having unique abilities that complement the other. The game’s narrative is deeply intertwined with its gameplay, using metaphors and imaginative settings to explore themes of love, communication, and teamwork. It Takes Two stands out for its creative design, humor, and heartwarming story, making it a memorable experience for players.

    It Takes Two received widespread acclaim for its co-op-centric gameplay and was lauded as one of the best multiplayer games of its time. The game’s ability to bring players together through a shared narrative experience, along with its charming visuals and varied gameplay, has made it a standout title in the cooperative gaming genre.

    Release Date: March 25, 2021

    Game of the Year: 2021

    Minimum System Requirements:

    • OS: Windows 8.1/10 (64-bit)
    • Processor: Intel Core i3-2100T or AMD FX 6100
    • Memory: 8 GB RAM
    • Graphics: NVIDIA GeForce GTX 660 or AMD R7 260x
    • DirectX: Version 11
    • Storage: 50 GB available space

    Recommended System Requirements:

    • OS: Windows 8.1/10 (64-bit)
    • Processor: Intel Core i5 3570K or AMD Ryzen 3 1300x
    • Memory: 16 GB RAM
    • Graphics: NVIDIA GeForce GTX 980 or AMD R9 290X
    • DirectX: Version 11
    • Storage: 50 GB available space

  • The Last of Us Part II ( PS4 & PS5 Exclusive )

    The Last of Us Part II is a critically acclaimed action-adventure game developed by Naughty Dog and published by Sony Interactive Entertainment. Released in June 2020, the game is a direct sequel to the 2013 hit The Last of Us, continuing the story of Ellie and Joel in a post-apocalyptic world ravaged by a fungal infection that turns people into zombie-like creatures. Set several years after the events of the first game, The Last of Us Part II follows a more mature Ellie as she embarks on a journey of revenge, grappling with the complex themes of trauma, loss, and the cycle of violence.

    The game is renowned for its intense and emotional narrative, which pushes the boundaries of storytelling in video games. Naughty Dog's attention to detail is evident in the realistic character animations, intricate environments, and nuanced performances by the voice actors. The gameplay builds on the foundations of the original, combining stealth, combat, and exploration with a heavy emphasis on crafting and survival. The game’s world is expansive, offering players a mix of linear and open-ended areas to explore, each filled with environmental storytelling and challenging enemies.

    Despite its accolades, The Last of Us Part II sparked considerable debate among fans and critics, particularly regarding its narrative choices and the brutal portrayal of violence. However, it remains a significant achievement in gaming, demonstrating how video games can convey complex emotions and moral ambiguity in a way that resonates deeply with players.

    Release Date: June 19, 2020

    Game of the Year: 2020

    As of now, The Last of Us Part II is exclusive to PlayStation 4 and PlayStation 5, and there is no official PC release.
